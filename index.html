About Tyler

Tyler, The Creator, born Tyler Gregory Okonma on March 6, 1991, is a rapper, producer, and fashion designer from Los Angeles. He gained fame as the leader of the hip-hop collective Odd Future and has since evolved into a groundbreaking solo artist.

Known for albums like Flower Boy and IGOR, Tyler blends genres and pushes creative boundaries. He also runs the successful clothing brand Golf Wang and hosts the Camp Flog Gnaw Carnival, cementing his influence beyond music.
